# Subly Privacy Policy

**Effective date: July 14, 2026**

Subly is a subscription tracker built to be private by design. This policy explains what data the app handles — which is very little — and what leaves your device.

## The short version

- Subly has **no accounts and no sign-in**.
- Everything you enter is stored **only on your device**.
- We run **no analytics, no ads, and no tracking** of any kind.
- We never see your subscriptions, your spending, or anything else you enter.

## Data you enter

Subscriptions, prices, billing dates, budgets, and settings are saved in a local database on your device. This data is never transmitted to us or to anyone else. It is included in your device backups according to your device's backup settings (e.g. iCloud or Google backup), which are governed by Apple's or Google's own policies.

You can delete all data at any time via **Settings → Delete all data**, or by uninstalling the app.

## Network requests the app makes

Subly works offline. It makes three kinds of outbound requests, none of which include your personal data or the contents of your subscription list as a whole:

1. **Service logos** — to display a subscription's logo, the app requests the logo image from Logo.dev (logo.dev) using that service's public domain name (e.g. `netflix.com`). Like any web request, this exposes your IP address to Logo.dev, along with the domain of the logo being fetched.
2. **Exchange rates** — the app fetches current EUR/USD exchange rates from the Frankfurter API (frankfurter.dev), a free, open service using European Central Bank data. This request contains no personal data.
3. **Fonts** — the app may download its typeface from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com) on first run. This request contains no personal data beyond your IP address.

If you use the app fully offline, none of these requests occur and the app remains functional (letter avatars replace logos, cached exchange rates are used).

## Notifications

Renewal and trial reminders are **local notifications**, scheduled entirely on your device. No notification data leaves your device, and no push-notification service is used.

## Import and export

Importing (CSV or pasted text) reads only the file or text you explicitly choose. Exporting creates a CSV file on your device and hands it to the system share sheet — where it goes from there is entirely your choice.

## What we collect about you

Nothing. We have no servers, no user database, and no way to identify you.

## Children

Subly does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children. The app is suitable for general audiences.

## Changes to this policy

If a future version of Subly changes how data is handled (for example, adding optional cloud sync), this policy will be updated and the effective date revised before that version ships.
